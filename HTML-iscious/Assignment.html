What Does The LawnCare Calculator Do?

1: Determine the most accurate way to calculate the area of the lawn that will be fertilized.

2: Calculate the ares of the lawn based on user data input.

3: Determine Lawn specific details (type of grass).

4: Consumer will enter the numbers that are listed on the N-P-K label of the fertilizer of their choice.

5: The weight of the fertilizer bag will also be entered by the consumer.

6: The LawnCare Calculator Tool then takes this information and formulates a custom LawnCare Rx.

7: Follow the recommendations provided via the LawnCare Rx that are customized to your lawns specific needs to insure your lawn's nutritional requirements are fulfilled.

8: Watch as your lawn begins to transform and you reap the reward of a beautifully healthy lawn.

The Result:

A Custom LawnCare Rx based on the User's specific LawnCare needs.

This Includes:

a. The Yearly Nitrogen Supplimentation Recommendation for the type of grass to be fertilized

b. How many applications it will take to meet the recommended supplimentation with the fertilizer they have chosen

c. How many bags of fertilizer total they will need to complete the whole process for the yearly dosage of nitrogen and other nutrients

d. How many bags of fertilizer they will need for the first application
