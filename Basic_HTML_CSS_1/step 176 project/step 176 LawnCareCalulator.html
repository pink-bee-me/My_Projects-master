 so the next question is...,
"What Does The LawnCare Calculator Do?" and "How can It Help Me?"

  The LawnCare Calculator:

  1. Determines the most accurate way to calculate the area of the lawn that will be fertilized.
  2. Calculates the ares of the lawn based on user data input.
  3. Evaluates Your Personal LawnCare Needs(based on the type of grass).
  4. Determines Nitrogen content of the fertilizer of User's choice based on the N-P-K label of the fertilizer(via user input)
  5. Then The weight of the fertilizer bag will also be entered by the consumer.
  6. Finally, The LawnCare Calculator takes ALL this information and formulates a custom LawnCare Rx for YOUR LAWN.

 What's Next...

 A Custom LawnCare Rx tailor-made for You!!!
 ... a personal LawnCare Prescription to transform YOUR
  lawn based on the needs of your personal lawn.


 YOUR Personal LawnCare Rx Includes:

  1. The Yearly Nitrogen Supplimentation Recommendation for the User's Type of Grass
  2. How many applications needed to fulfill the recommended nutritional supplimentation requirements based on the chosen fertilizer
  3. How many bags of fertilizer total needed to complete the whole process for the yearly dosage of nitrogen and other nutrients
  4. How many bags of fertilizer needed to complete the first application
